Golkonda

Golkonda, a historic fortress and citadel located in Hyderabad, India, is a testament to the region's rich cultural and architectural heritage. Originally built during the Kakatiya dynasty in the 13th century, it was later fortified and expanded by the Qutb Shahi dynasty, reaching its peak during the 16th and 17th centuries.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a revered Sikh gurdwara located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It was founded by the fourth Sikh Guru, Guru Ram Das, in the 16th century, and completed in 1604 by Guru Arjan Dev, the fifth Sikh Guru. The temple's striking architecture features a gilded exterior, which gives it its iconic name.
